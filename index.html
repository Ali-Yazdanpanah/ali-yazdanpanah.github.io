About

I’m a systems engineer and researcher working at the intersection of high-performance computing, cloud, and applied AI. My work is measurement-first and reproducibility-driven: I study how computation behaves on heterogeneous processors and large clusters, then turn those insights into dependable speedups. I’m especially interested in ML for systems using learning to guide scheduling, optimization, and experimentation, and in how hardware-aware design, security, and even quantum ideas can push scientific and reliability goals forward.
